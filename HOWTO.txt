To start the project:

1. Make sure no other service is running on port 5000
2. For Mac user, go to /MacOS/app and click on app.exe
3. For Windows user, go to /Windows/app and click on app.exe
4. visit 127.0.0.1:5000/login with your browser

* Note: If you are in China, you may either need to use a VPN or change the CDN service in the HTML templates
